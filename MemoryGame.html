Welcome to the Memory Game!

In this game you have five pairs of pictures that you have to remember. If you pick a matching pair, your strikes will be increased by one. When you have two strikes, you get one life. If you pick unmatching pair, your lives will be decreased by one and your strikes start from the beginning.

You can win the game by picking all the matching pictures. You lose the game if your lives drop to zero.

Clock is ticking! Try to get the best time!
